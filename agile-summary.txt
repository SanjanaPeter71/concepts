Agile: 
    1. Iterative approach to project management & software development.
    2. Helps team to deliver work in small consumable and faster approach.
    3. Requirements, plans and results are evaluated continuously.
    4. It calls for collaboration, adaptation among team members.

Principles
- Iterative, incremental, and evolutionary
- Efficient and face-to-face communication
- Very short feedback loop and adaptation cycle
- Quality focus

Pitfalls
- Lack of overall product design
- Adding stories to an iteration in progress
- Insufficient training
- Developer burnout
- Fixed time, resources, scope, and Quality
- Attempting to take on too much in an iteration
